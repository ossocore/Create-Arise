DESCRIPTION
Create: Arise is a semi-expert Modpack centered around the create mod and its addons, in particular create: Aeronautics - which allows player to build Aircrafts of various types aswell as featuring a very robust physics engine. It's main appeal is a strong focus on progression aswell as use of various create mod mechanics within the progression, making the progression feel integrated with included mods.

The player will build need to progress from sticks and stones to building airships at a slowed down pace compared to vanilla and quite a few recipes are a lot more expensive to push the player into automating resources with the Create Mod. At each milestone the player will now be able to craft recipes that improve quality of life - such as better storage systems, more transport options or getting stronger through gear.

Mobs are also enhanced to supplement the survival gameplay and give a good reason for players to persue getting stronger equipment using the Silent Gear mod.

DETAILS
Changes from vanilla behaviour:
- Nether requires a catalyst to be opened which, amongst other things, needs diamonds
- Diamonds don't naturally spawn or are found in chests, instead there is a new fluid found in sky islands called molten opal, which needs to be processed into Diamonds
- The players cant build in a certain band of blocks in the sky - forcing them to make planes/airships etc to reach sky islands and collect the molten opal
- molten opal will need to be gathered in vast amounts - the expectation here is that the player are gonna want to build a flying tanker airship, sucking up the liquid using hose pullies or similar from create

STAGES OF PROGRESSION
- ultra early game with the "tough beginnings" mod
- stone to copper equipment stages are defined by overgeared
- when unlocking copper tools you can now get your first iron - which will be the gateway to create
- after making a mechanical press this unlocks the stone anvil from Silent Gear - unlocking Iron tools
- After Iron tools the player will want to start progressing towards the nether. The nether requires a Catalyst to open which needs diamonds
- the player now needs to progress further into create and start making planes and so on
- the physics assembler, which is needed to make any Aeronautics contraption, will be a lot more expensive to make, effectivly acting as another gate and enforcing some automation
- after aquiring diamonds and such players will need to process them and then after crafting the catalyst will be able to enter the nether
- the nether now enables players to enter the brass age in create aswell as a lot better equipment
- [end of current planing] we want to make a similar ish gating for the end as we did the nether - however there needs to be more progression after unlocking the end to persue, otherwise it wouldnt make too much sense. BEATING THE ENDER DRAGON WILL NOT BE THE END OF THE MODPACK!!
